# My Daily Routine

## Morning
- I woke up in the morning.
- I offered my prayer.
- I got ready for university.
- I went to the university bus stop.
- I boarded the bus and reached the university.
- I attended my lectures.

## Afternoon
- At 12:00 PM, I went to the café.
- I spent enjoyable time with my friends.
- At 1:00 PM, I attended another lecture.

## Evening
- At 4:00 PM, I went back to the bus stop.
- I took the bus and returned home.
- I rested after reaching home.
- At 9:00 PM, I woke up and had dinner.
- I prepared for my quiz.
- Then I went to sleep.
